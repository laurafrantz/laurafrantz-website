We celebrated the life of a loved one this past weekend at the bay. Before stepping into the knee-deep water to take pictures, I pulled the bottom hem of my long grey skirt up between my legs and tucked it into the front of my waistband (think [Lucy meets grapes](http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-kpdZUn39Yvg/Tj2lBeIwSSI/AAAAAAAAAyc/z7UW4LhGykE/s1600/lucy+grape+stomp2.jpg)). The flowers were so beautiful floating on the glassy surface. I followed them across the cove for a few minutes, watching the ripples from my movements gently rock each petaled head. After the sun set, the lanterns filled slowly with fire-warmed air and pulled away into the purple sky, flying in her memory and honor, while her flowers drifted in the tide below.
