It is kind of weird to be shopping at Whole Foods on a Sunday afternoon and come across [a band](http://www.mixedbusinessmusic.com/Index.html) standing in a little triangle-shaped space between the olive oil, fish fry counter and bulk dried beans, jamming their faces off. It’s even weirder when the music is actually incredibly good. I wedged my body between two parties of greasy-lipped diners woofing down their fried oysters to take these shots, but in my defense, I did give them the apologetic photog face.
