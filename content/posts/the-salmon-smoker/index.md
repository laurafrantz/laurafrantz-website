Sunday afternoon I watched [this](https://vimeo.com/fvonf/ole-hansen) short film about a life with singular purpose. His goal was to recreate a flavor from his childhood, and in the process he crafted a unique place for himself in this world. He sleeps in a tiny cabin built on the roof of his business, writes ideas and plans into little notebooks, carves away fins with surgical precision. I ache to carve away excess in my life, to find my singular purpose.
