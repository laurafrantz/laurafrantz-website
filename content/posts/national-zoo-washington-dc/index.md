Things I learned this day:

+ I can finally get my hair back into a ponytail, and that is where it shall stay for the hot Virginia summertime.

+ Metro escalator repair men will sometimes stop you to talk cameras and lenses at the Woodley Park exit.

+ Diplomatic motorcades are really, really loud when they drive by and they go stupid fast, which you would think would defeat the purpose of not drawing unwelcome attention to the occupants of the vehicles. But they're the pros and I am just a mom.

+ When one elephant pees, the others in its enclosure tend to follow suit. The trainers call it a "pee party". Now I can check elephant pee party off of my list of things to see before I die.

+ Riding the Metro as part of a posse of five kids and two moms with a stroller and a giant backpack is much more fun than riding the Metro as a single working woman wearing ear buds and clutching a briefcase.
