I sewed a Japanese-style cross-back apron out of Baltic linen for a very beautiful and dear friend of mine. I was in such a hurry to ship it off to her that I didn’t bother to take any photos, but she’s graciously allowing me to post her pics here. I used the instructions found [here](http://www.thedailysew.com/2015/02/japanese-style-smock-apron-to-sew/) to piece it together in just a couple of hours. Then I slipped a quilted heart into the oversized pocket for extra love.
