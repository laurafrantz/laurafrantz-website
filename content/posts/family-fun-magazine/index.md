Disney's Family Fun Magazine featured the bed cave curtains and chalk board wall from our old house in New York in their May 2010 issue ([click here for the PDF](http://laurafrantz.com/docs/frantzffmay2010.pdf)). My favorite part: the freakishly talented illustrator of the piece, [Clare Mallison](http://www.claremallison.com/), referenced [this](http://www.flickr.com/photos/laurafrantz/3304428162/in/set-72157613019968531/) picture of one of the bed caves to create a cute, dare I say whimsical little illustration of my girl reading in her beddie. Soooo fun, especially the (imaginary) drawings of butterflies and unicorns on the wall.
