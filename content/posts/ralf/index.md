The first time I visited the website of accomplished Stockholm-based illustrator [Ingela P. Arrhenius](http://www.ingelaparrhenius.com/), she was coincidentally having a giveaway. I entered, and to my great surprise I won this studly little Swedish man. He now sits on our nature table and watches over the children as they do their homework. He has found a compatriot in my grandfather’s Dala horse.
