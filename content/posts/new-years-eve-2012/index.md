![31 dec 2012](image-01.jpg)

Spent the morning with my loves at a [cozy coffee shop](http://northsidesocialarlington.com/) in Arlington. I wrote in my journal about 2013, about my hopes for renewal and rebirth. My girl began writing in her [tiny purple Moleskine](http://www.amazon.com/Moleskine-Notebook-Purple-Legendary-Notebooks/dp/8862938454/), too. Tonight we will light candles, share our writings and craft a family vision for this year to come. Then we will pop popcorn and play a game, and then we will blow out the candles and nestle down into our covers. And when we wake, it will be a new year.
