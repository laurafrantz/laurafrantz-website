My interrupted photographs illustrate the Winter 2015 issue of online literary nonfiction journal [Brevity Magazine](https://brevitymag.com/category/issues/issue-48-winter-2015/). I clucked over this project like a mother hen, and gave it the majority of my spare time in November and December, to the dismay of those I love. Now this project is out in the world, and I bend to the task of redemption.
