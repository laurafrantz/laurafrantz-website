I turned the [surprise cucumbers](https://laurafrantz.com//wordpress/2013/07/surprise.html) into [sweet and spicy pickles](http://www.marthastewart.com/316122/sweet-and-spicy-bread-and-butter-pickles) this weekend. Let me just say, pickling is far easier than fermenting. No vegetal whey taste, no wondering about exactly what kind of microbe I am growing, and the cukes were ready to eat two hours after hitting the vinegar and spice. They are delicious. I am eating them with everything, at every meal, and dreaming about making my next batch as soon as we can locate more vegetables in the jungle out back.
