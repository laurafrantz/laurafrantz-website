My gift wrapping this year started with a walk in the woods to gather bits and ended with brown paper packages tied up with string. All of our gifts were handmade, out of necessity and also just because. I’m feeling super-inspired by [Mary Jo Hoffman’s STILL project](http://stillblog.net/) lately, so much so that I just ordered a plate for my tripod, and have plans to finally learn how to use my bounce flash once and for all.

Oh, and that last gift is a bottle of my daughter’s favorite barbecue sauce that the local stores have inexplicably stopped carrying. Because that is how I roll.
