![yellow eye stuebens](image-01.jpg)

I catch sight of them in the store and stop in my tracks. I stand in front of the wooden bin for a long time and use the thick metal scoop to lift and pour pile after pile, watching the cream, mustard and burgundy spin down. I say over and over again in a soft voice (to no one in particular) that these beans,

*these beans right here*

are the most beautiful beans I’ve ever seen.
